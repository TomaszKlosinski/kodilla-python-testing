About

My name is Tomasz Klosinski and I'm a DevOps/Site Reliability Engineer & Python Developer based in Zurich, Switzerland.

I'm originally from Poland. I started my career in Warsaw, Poland in 2011 as a Linux (RHEL) System Administrator, but in about a year I moved away from pure server administration to work with Cloud platforms and DevOps automation tools.

I moved to Switzerland in 2014, where I started working at CERN in Geneva, in the French-speaking part of the country. It was a one-year contract as a Project Associate. I was responsible for modernizing the automation and configuration management stack in the team that was responsible for over 2000 Linux machines operating Large Hadron Collider.

After that I moved to Zug (near Zurich) in German-speaking Switzerland to work for a new FinTech bank. Since then, I have continued working in the Swiss FinTech industry.
